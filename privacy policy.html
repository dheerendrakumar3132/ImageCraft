Privacy Policy

Your creative assets are your business. Keeping them private is ours.

Effective Date: January 2026

At ImageCraft Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This document describes the types of information we may collect and our practices for handling that data.

1. The "Zero-Knowledge" Architecture

The most critical pillar of ImageCraft Pro is our Local-First Processing model. Unlike traditional online editors, we do not utilize server-side rendering for your images. Every single pixel manipulation, stitching, and merging process happens within your browser's sandboxed environment.

What this means for you:

  • Your raw images never touch our servers.
  • We cannot see, access, or store your photos.
  • There is no database where your personal creative assets are kept.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Because our tool is designed for maximum privacy, we purposefully avoid collecting the following:

  • Personal Identity: We do not ask for names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • User Metadata: We do not extract EXIF data (location, camera settings) from your uploaded photos.
  • Image History: Once you close your browser tab, all session data is permanently erased from your RAM.

3. Minimal Data Usage

We only collect limited, non-identifying information necessary to maintain site stability and improve the user experience:

  • Device Metrics: Screen resolution and browser version to ensure the UI renders correctly.
  • Performance Logs: Anonymous data regarding tool errors to help us fix bugs.
  • Traffic Source: General information about how you found us (e.g., via a search engine) for SEO purposes.

4. Third-Party Advertising & Cookies

To provide this professional tool free of charge, we may display advertisements through partners like Google AdSense. These partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visit history. Note that these advertisers have zero access to the images you process within our tool.

5. Data Security Measures

While we don't store your images, we protect your connection to our site using Industry-Standard SSL/TLS Encryption. This ensures that your interaction with the ImageCraft Pro interface is secure from middleman attacks.

6. Your Rights & Control

You have the right to clear your browser cache and cookies at any time to remove any local traces of your session settings. Since we hold no personal data on our servers, you do not need to request data deletion—you are always in full control of your local environment.

7. Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact our privacy compliance officer through the official contact channels provided on our website.

Our Privacy Promise

We will never sell your data, we will never track your images, and we will always prioritize your security over our profits.
