Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use small data to deliver a better creative experience.

Last Revised: January 2026

At ImageCraft Pro, we believe in being clear and open about how we collect and use data related to you.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are sent to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store some information about your preferences or past actions.

2. How ImageCraft Pro Uses Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience and to understand how our tool is being used. Specifically, we use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Functional Cookies: These are essential for the website to remember your tool settings, such as whether you prefer a Horizontal or Vertical layout for your merged images.
  • Analytical Cookies: We use aggregated data to monitor site traffic and performance. This helps us identify technical issues and understand which features are most valuable to our users.
  • Preference Cookies: These help the site remember your choices (like your UI theme preferences) to provide a more personalized experience on your next visit.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the service and deliver advertisements through partners like Google AdSense. These third parties may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

4. Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted (for example, your tool preferences may not be saved).

5. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit allaboutcookies.org.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please re-visit this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.

Your Control

By continuing to use our service without changing your settings, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
