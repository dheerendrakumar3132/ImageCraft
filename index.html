Frequently Asked Questions

How does ImageCraft ensure my privacy?

All image processing happens directly in your web browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring the complete privacy and security of your data.

Is there a limit to the number of images I can merge?

No, there is no hard limit! You can merge as many images as your browser's memory can handle. For most modern devices, this means processing dozens of images without issue.

Will my image quality be reduced after merging?

Absolutely not. ImageCraft merges your photos at their original resolution, ensuring there is zero loss in quality. Your final output will be just as sharp and detailed as your input images.

Can I merge images vertically instead of horizontally?

Yes! Our tool provides a simple dropdown option in the main interface to switch between horizontal (side-by-side) and vertical (stacked) merging directions.
